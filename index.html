About Her

Yamini Krishnamurthy, an iconic figure in Indian classical dance, is renowned for her mastery of Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi. Her journey in dance began at Kalakshetra, where she trained rigorously to become a symbol of artistic excellence.

With grace and precision, she brought classical dance to the global stage, inspiring countless artists with her expressive performances and innovative choreography.

Awards & Recognition

  • Padma Vibhushan (2016)
  • Padma Bhushan (2001)
  • Padma Shri (1968)
  • Sangeet Natak Akademi Award
